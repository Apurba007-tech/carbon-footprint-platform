High-impact actions you can take today

Switch car trips to rail

Replacing a 500 km car journey with train travel cuts emissions by up to 85%.

Save ~0.5–2.0 t/yr

Switch to renewable electricity

A green electricity tariff or rooftop solar eliminates one of the largest household emission sources.

Save ~0.8–1.5 t/yr

Cut one long-haul flight

A single round-trip transatlantic flight emits more CO₂ than many countries' residents produce in a year.

Shift to a plant-rich diet

Cutting meat to 3 days a week reduces your diet footprint by nearly 40%.

A small temperature adjustment combined with better insulation cuts heating bills meaningfully.

Save ~0.2–0.4 t/yr

Choosing second-hand goods avoids the embedded manufacturing emissions entirely.

Save ~0.1–0.3 t/yr
