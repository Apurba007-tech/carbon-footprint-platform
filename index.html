Reduce your footprint

High-impact actions you can take today

Ranked by tonnes of CO₂ saved per year on average.

🚆
Switch car trips to rail

Replacing a 500 km car journey with train travel cuts emissions by up to 85%. Work or holiday trips are the easiest wins.

Save ~0.5–2.0 t/yr
⚡
Switch to renewable electricity

Choosing a green electricity tariff or installing rooftop solar eliminates one of the largest household emission sources.

Save ~0.8–1.5 t/yr
✈️
Cut one long-haul flight

A single round-trip transatlantic flight emits more CO₂ than most countries' residents produce in a year. Go by sea or stay closer.

Save ~1.5–3.0 t per trip
🥗
Shift to a plant-rich diet

Cutting meat to just 3 days a week reduces your diet footprint by nearly 40%. Going fully plant-based saves over 1.5 t/yr.

Save ~0.5–1.5 t/yr
🌡️
Lower your thermostat 2°C

A small temperature adjustment combined with better draught-proofing and insulation cuts heating bills and emissions meaningfully.

Save ~0.2–0.4 t/yr
♻️
Buy second-hand first

Manufacturing new goods is carbon intensive. Choosing second-hand clothing, electronics, and furniture avoids the embedded emissions entirely.

Save ~0.1–0.3 t/yr
